All Your Startup’s Vesting Schedule Needs in One Simple Tool

Your startup’s vesting schedule is a key component of your growing business. Everyone from angel and venture capital investors to consultants and employees want regular access to a professional and up-to-date vesting schedule. IncTrak is an all-in-one vesting management tool that allows you to quickly and easily maintain an accurate record and schedule of all your startup’s stock and options disbursements, and present the results in an intuitive and visually-appealing format. IncTrak also has a number of additional vesting management features for maximizing the tool’s functionality, with more great features being added all the time!

Make Your Strongest Incentives Shine
